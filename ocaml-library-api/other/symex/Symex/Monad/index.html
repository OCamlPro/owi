Module Symex.Monad

A monad representing computation that can be cooperatively scheduled. Computations can yield (Yield), and split into two non deterministic choices (Choice). They can also fail/stop (Ok versus Error).

type ('a, 'err, 'prio, 'state) schedulable =
  1. | Ok of 'a * 'state
  2. | Error of 'err
  3. | Yield of 'prio * unit -> ('a, 'err, 'prio, 'state) schedulable
  4. | Choice of ('a, 'err, 'prio, 'state) schedulable * ('a, 'err, 'prio, 'state) schedulable
